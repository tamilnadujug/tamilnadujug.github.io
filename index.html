About Tamilnadu JUG

Tamil JUG is a unique Java User Group with a special focus on bringing Java to students and fresh engineering professionals. Drawing from our experiences with various JUGs, our working model prioritizes students and community growth.

We provide a forum to share ideas, discuss, and promote Java and JVM technologies. Anyone with an interest in Java & JVM is encouraged to join. Our meetings include tech talks, workshops, and code jams on various topics related to Java.

Our Mission & Approach

Campus Meetups

We conduct JUG meetups directly at college campuses, making them accessible to students who cannot reach major cities due to location and financial constraints.

Building Leaders

Each college nominates one male and one female student as Chapter Leads. These leaders organize and manage meetups, fostering a community of Java enthusiasts.

Enabling Teachers

Teachers play a crucial role in connecting with students. We run special programs to enable teachers to effectively teach Java and mentor students.

Open Source

We promote Java-based open source projects among students and teachers, providing them with real-world experiences and opportunities to contribute.
